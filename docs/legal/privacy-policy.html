Stepper Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 9, 2026

Stepper ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile fitness application for tracking daily steps, competing with friends, and joining groups. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

Health & Fitness Data

  • Step count data from Apple Health (HealthKit) on iOS and Health Connect on Android.
  • We read step count data to display your daily progress and enable competitions with friends.
  • Health data is used solely within the app for its core functionality and is never sold to third parties.

Account Information

  • Email address and display name when you create an account.
  • Authentication is handled securely through Supabase Auth.

Usage Data

  • We may collect anonymized analytics to improve app performance and user experience.
  • This does not include personally identifiable health data.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Display your daily step count and progress.
  • Enable step competitions and leaderboards with friends and groups.
  • Send notifications about competitions, friend requests, and milestones.
  • Improve the app experience through anonymized analytics.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL) with encryption at rest and in transit.
  • Health data accessed via HealthKit remains on your device and is only synced as step counts to our servers for competition features.
  • We do not store raw HealthKit data on our servers beyond aggregated step counts.

4. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell your personal data or health data to any third party.
  • Your step count data is shared only with friends and group members you have explicitly connected with in the app.
  • We may share anonymized, aggregated data for analytics purposes.

5. HealthKit & Health Connect

  • We access Apple HealthKit and Android Health Connect solely to read step count data.
  • We do not write data back to HealthKit or Health Connect.
  • HealthKit and Health Connect data is not used for advertising or marketing.
  • You can revoke health data access at any time through your device settings.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all data we hold about you within the app.
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data by contacting us.
  • Revoke permissions: You can revoke health data access through your device's Settings at any time.

7. Children's Privacy

Stepper is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app.
